1. Introduction

WordLegend ("App") is a mobile word game designed to improve your vocabulary and word skills. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our App.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • User ID (UID) from Firebase Authentication
  • Email address (if you choose to sign in with email)
  • Game progress: level, words solved, current streak, best streak, earned badges
  • Played words (to prevent repetition)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics and analytics
  • Advertisement interaction data (via Google AdMob)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game functionality
  • To save your progress, levels, streaks, and badges
  • To deliver a personalized gaming experience
  • To improve the App and develop new features
  • To analyze advertisement performance
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Your Information

We may share your information with the following third parties:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and analytics services
  • Google AdMob: Advertising and ad performance measurement
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Advertisements and Cookies

The free version of the App displays third-party advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies and similar technologies. You can remove all advertisements by subscribing to Premium.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. Users under 13 are not allowed to use the App. Users aged 13-18 must have parental or guardian consent.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no security system is completely infallible.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction or deletion of your data
  • Objection to the processing of your data
  • Portability of your data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via in-app notification or email. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy.
