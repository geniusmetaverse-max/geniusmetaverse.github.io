Who is WordLegend?

WordLegend is a modern and addictive word game developed for word enthusiasts. Our goal is to improve people’s vocabulary in a fun way, contribute to the effective use of the Turkish language, and provide enjoyable moments for players of all ages.

Why WordLegend?

Most Turkish word games on the market are either too simple or too difficult. We have created the perfect balance between these two extremes.

With its smart difficulty system ranging from 3 to 6 letters, elegant design, smooth interface, and premium quality, WordLegend is not just a game — it’s a journey through words.

Our Vision

To become the most preferred Turkish word game in Turkey and around the world, and to bring people together through the love of words.

Our Values

  • Quality & Simplicity — Not complicated, just fun and smooth.
  • Respect for Turkish Language — We take pride in our language.
  • Continuous Improvement — We value user feedback.
  • Fair Play — Everyone should play under equal conditions.

Who Are We?

WordLegend was brought to life by a small but passionate team of developers and designers who are deeply in love with word games. Our goal is big: to make people happy through words.
