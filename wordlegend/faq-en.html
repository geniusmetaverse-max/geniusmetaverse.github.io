Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is WordLegend? +
WordLegend is a modern word guessing game designed to improve your vocabulary and have fun. You can play with new words every day, ranging from 3 to 6 letters, with different game modes.
What are the Game Modes? +
To make the word guessing experience more enjoyable, different modes are offered:

Standard Mode: Find the word within a time limit and 5 guesses. Progress levels and earn coins.
Hard Mode: Find the word within a time limit and only 1 guess. Progress levels and earn coins.
Timeless Mode: Find the word with 5 guesses and no time limit. No level or coin rewards.
Unlimited Mode: Find the word with no time or guess limits. No level or coin rewards.
Is the app completely free? +
Yes, you can download and enjoy the full gameplay for free. However, for users who dislike mandatory ads, WordLegend Pro offers lifetime access with an ad-free experience.
Are the words only in English? +
No. If you prefer, you can switch the app language to Turkish in the Settings and continue playing with Turkish words.
Is there a daily word limit? +
No. You can solve as many words as you want. However, there is a special “Daily Challenge” mode that can only be played once per day.
Is my progress saved in the cloud? +
Yes. When you sign in with Google or Apple, all your progress (levels, badges, streaks, solved words) is securely stored on Firebase and synced across devices.
How are the leaderboards calculated? +
Leaderboards on the Statistics page are calculated based on the number of games played and words correctly guessed, regardless of word length or game mode.
Why are there so many ads? +
Ads are shown in the free version to keep the game free. For an ad-free experience, you can purchase WordLegend Pro.
What does WordLegend Pro offer? +
With WordLegend Pro, all mandatory ads are removed. You also gain lifetime access to the app.
Can I cancel after purchasing the package? +
Purchased packages are one-time and cannot be canceled.
