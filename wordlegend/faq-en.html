Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is WordLegend? +
WordLegend is a modern word guessing game designed to improve your vocabulary and have fun. You can play with new words every day from 3 to 6 letters with increasing difficulty.
Is the app completely free? +
Yes, the core gameplay is completely free. However, Premium subscription is available for users who want to remove ads and unlock extra features.
What does Premium subscription offer? +
Premium membership removes all advertisements, gives unlimited hints, unlocks exclusive badges, and provides access to special themes and animations.
Is my progress saved on the cloud? +
Yes. When you sign in with Google or Apple, your entire progress (levels, badges, streaks, solved words) is securely saved on Firebase and synced across your devices.
Is there a daily word limit? +
No. You can solve as many words as you want. There is also a special “Daily Word” mode available every day.
Are the words only in Turkish? +
Yes. Currently, the word pool consists only of Turkish words. English language support may be added in future updates.
How can I delete my account? +
Go to Settings > Account > "Delete My Account". This action is permanent and will delete all your progress.
I have a technical issue, what should I do? +
Try restarting the app. If the problem continues, you can reach us through Settings > Support or by emailing support@wordlegend.app.
Why are there so many ads? +
Ads are shown in the free version to keep the game free. You can completely remove ads by purchasing a Premium subscription.
How can I contact WordLegend? +
For any questions, suggestions, or feedback, please email us at support@wordlegend.app. We will respond as soon as possible.
