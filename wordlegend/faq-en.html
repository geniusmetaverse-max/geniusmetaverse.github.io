Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is WordLegend? +
WordLegend is a modern word guessing game designed to improve your vocabulary and entertain you. Every day you can play with new words ranging from 3 to 6 letters in different word lengths and game modes.
What are the Game Modes? +
To make your word guessing experience more enjoyable, different game modes are offered:

Standard: Find the word within the given time and 5 guesses based on the chosen word length. Level up and earn points.
Hard: Find the word within the given time and only 1 guess based on the chosen word length. Level up and earn points.
Timeless: Find the word with 5 guesses and no time limit. No level or point rewards.
Unlimited: Find the word with no time or guess limits. No level or point rewards.
Is the app completely free? +
Yes, you can download the app and enjoy the full game experience for free. However, for users who are bothered by mandatory ads, the WordLegend Pro package offers lifetime usage and ad-free gameplay.
Are the words only in Turkish? +
No. If you wish, you can change the language preference to English in Settings/Language and continue playing with English words.
Is there a daily word play limit? +
No. You can solve as many words as you like. However, the “Daily Challenge” game can only be played once per day with the special word chosen for that day.
Is my progress saved? +
Yes. When you log in with your Google or Apple account, all your progress (levels, badges, streaks, solved words) is securely stored and synchronized across devices.
How are the leaderboards calculated? +
The rankings on the statistics page are calculated based on the number of games played and the number of correctly guessed words, regardless of the chosen word length or game mode.
Why are ads shown? +
Ads are shown in the free version to keep the game free. To continue playing without mandatory ads, you can purchase the WordLegend Pro package.
What does the WordLegend Pro package provide? +
With the WordLegend Pro package, all mandatory ads are removed. You also gain lifetime access to the app.
Can purchased packages be canceled? +
No, all packages are one-time purchases and cannot be canceled.
